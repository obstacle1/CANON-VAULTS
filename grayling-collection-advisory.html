Background

Thomas Grayling has spent over a decade managing significant private estates and collections for ultra-high-net-worth principals and family offices across California and New York. His hands-on experience spans fine art, vintage European automobiles, timepieces, Art Nouveau and Art Deco jewelry, wine, rare books, and architectural collections — with combined assets managed in excess of $100 million.

His editorial work — including a feature on the emerging luxury vault and private custody market published under his byline in March 2026 — reflects a practitioner's understanding of where private collection management is heading and what serious collectors actually need from the people they trust with their objects.

Grayling Collection Advisory clients benefit from access to Provenance, a proprietary collection intelligence platform developed to support valuation tracking, condition documentation, and advisor reporting — purpose-built for the private collector market.
